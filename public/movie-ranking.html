The Best Movies according to Manish

My top 3 movies of all time.

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)

> Epic fantasy directed by S.S. Rajamouli.
> Set new standards for Indian cinema with its scale and storytelling

RRR (2022)

> Another Rajamouli masterpiece starring N.T. Rama Rao Jr. and Ram Charan.
> A fictional tale of two revolutionaries fighting British colonial rule.
> Won the Oscar for Best Original Song (“Naatu Naatu”) and became a global sensation

777 Charlie (2022)

> A Kannada gem that tugs at the heartstrings with its emotional story of a man and his dog.
> Celebrated for its warmth and storytelling.
